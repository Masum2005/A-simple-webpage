The Indian Dinosaur

Found in India

Rajasaurus means "King Lizard" and was discovered in India.It lived during the Late Cretaceous period (~70 million years ago).

Fierce Predator

It was a carnivorous theropod that hunted other dinosaurs.It had sharp teeth and strong jaws for crushing bones.

Medium-Sized But Deadly

  • Length: 7-9 meters (23-30 feet)
  • Weight: 3-4 tons
  • Not as big as T-Rex but still a powerful predator.

Unique Skull Crest

It had a distinctive horn/crest on its skull.This made it look different from other theropods like T-Rex.

Lived in the Indian Subcontinent

Fossils were found in Gujarat, India (Narmada Valley).It lived in a land connected to Madagascar and Africa.The Gujarat state has declared the fossil site in Balasinor a "dinosaur park," sometimes called the "Jurassic Park of India" in reference to the 1993 film, in response to the massive rush of tourism, with the crowning attraction being Rajasaurus.In Rahioli, a life-sized statue was erected in honour of its discovery.
